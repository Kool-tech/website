About Us

Each One Educate One is dedicated to bridging educational gaps by connecting mentors with learners in underserved communities. We believe in the power of community-led learning and sustainable impact.

Our Programs

Mentorship

One-on-one mentorship pairing volunteer tutors with eager learners.

After‑School Tutoring

Support sessions for students in math, science, and language arts.

Digital Literacy

Classes and workshops teaching essential computer and digital skills.
